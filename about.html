Who I Am

Background

I don't have impressive credentials. I'm not affiliated with any institution or ideology. I'm just someone trying to think clearly about the world and share what I find in case it helps someone else see things more clearly too.

Why I Write

The honest reason

Because most writing is either too academic to be useful or too dumbed down to be meaningful. Because I'm frustrated with systems that don't serve people. Because writing forces me to think clearly. And because occasionally someone tells me that something I wrote helped them understand something better, and that makes it worthwhile.

What to Expect

I write like a human being, not a content generator. I have opinions and I defend them with evidence. I admit uncertainty when I'm uncertain. I swear when it's appropriate. I care more about being honest than being polite to institutions that deserve criticism.

I don't post on a schedule. I write when I have something worth saying. Quality over quantity, substance over noise.

If you're looking for algorithmic content optimized for engagement, you're in the wrong place. If you want someone to think through ideas honestly and explain them clearly, welcome.
