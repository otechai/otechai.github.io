What You Can Learn From This

If you're trying to sell Odoo to companies like Medidis, understand:

You're not selling software. You're selling risk reduction.

To win, you need:

Stop talking about "flexibility" and "open source."

Talk about:

- Risk mitigation strategies
- Compliance maintenance
- Export documentation
- Production efficiency

2. Show Similar Success

One case study of a furniture manufacturer using Odoo successfully is worth more than 100 features comparisons.

Find one. If you can't find one, create one.

3. Build the Morocco-Specific Modules

- "Proof of concept for new factory"
